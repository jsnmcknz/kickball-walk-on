6 — Shareable scorecard (S3 renderer target)

Batting order snakes across columns (staircase) — blank cells mean the order didn’t reach that player that inning. Diamond edges fill as bases are reached (lower-right = 1st, upper-right = 2nd, upper-left = 3rd); solid diamond = run scored; plum ✕ = runner erased at that base, hit stands (Kirsten, 3rd); shaded ? = missed play, excluded from rate stats (†); corner triangle = third out. Rendered to canvas at 2× and shared as PNG via the share button (app chrome, not part of the image).

Flag: scoring.flyLocationField (default true). The picker IS the diamond zone — runner chips stay live (a runner tap opens the normal runner sheet; the picker returns after it resolves). Dismisses via zone tap, skip, ~6s timeout, or any other tap winning — it never captures input, so the next result tap always executes normally. Location logs as an amend on the FLY event; a dismissed fly can still get one via fix-last. FLY only — no GND/K capture. Everything below the prompt stays fully live, never dimmed.
